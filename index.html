Our story

Built by people who know the journey.

We've been on both sides of the gap.

One of us came to the UK as an international student, chasing a better future but quickly realised how overwhelming and unfair the journey could be. Despite the hard work, talented people kept getting overlooked.

The other spent over a decade inside finance, sales and recruitment, working with companies of all sizes and seeing first-hand how difficult it was for them to find the right people — not because the talent wasn't out there, but because the system didn't connect the dots.

So we built Advance Group to change that. To prepare and empower ambitious people, and to help businesses find, hire and grow the talent they need to move forward.
